About Me –


Hi, I’m Justine a freelance UX / UI Designer with a passion for design and all things visual.


My strengths are found in creative, critical and liminal thinking and empathy for the human experience. My curious nature allows for understanding, shaping and reframing of assumptions; taking what is learnt and what is habit and pushing for better more intuitive user focused outcomes.


These honed skills give me the ability to thrive in human centred design and with a background in graphic design I have a strong understanding of the visual language and how to effectively communicate for the benefit of both the client and user.


Adaptable and reliable, I work proficiently in a team or independently to achieve project deadlines. I have experience working with existing clients, using style guides and developing new brands, with special interest in the innovative space.


Creating solutions that give clarity to innovators and build authentic connections with audiences.



SKILLS

Listening, observing, testing, analysing, problem solving, implementing — repeat.


TOOLS

Adobe CC - Photoshop, XD, Illustrator, Indesign. Figma, Miro, Trello.
